# Tier 1
## Backswing Cut
1/round, when you hit with a Heavy or Superheavy melee weapon, you can make another melee attack with the same weapon as a free action against a different character within Threat and line of sight. This attack deals half damage, if successful.

---
# Tier 2
## Wide Arc Cleave
The first time in a round that you perform a critical hit with a Heavy or Superheavy melee weapon, you deal 3 Kinetic damage to all characters and objects of your choice within Threat, other than the one you just attacked.

---
# Tier 3
## No Escape
1/round, when you miss with a melee attack, you reroll it against a different target within Threat and line of sight.
